What is obesity?

More than body weight

Obesity is a health condition with excess body fat that may increase the risk of diabetes, blood pressure issues, heart disease, and reduced physical fitness.

What is adolescent obesity?

Growth stage with special risk

During teenage years, rapid physical and hormonal changes make nutrition, sleep, physical activity, and screen-time habits especially important for lifelong health.

Health Risks

Dangers & Side Effects of Obesity

Cardiovascular Diseases

High blood pressure, heart disease, and stroke risk increase dramatically with excess body weight.

Type 2 Diabetes

Obesity is the primary risk factor for developing insulin resistance and diabetes complications.

Joint & Bone Problems

Extra weight stresses joints, leading to arthritis, back pain, and mobility issues.

Sleep Disorders

Sleep apnea and poor sleep quality affect cognitive development and academic performance.

Mental Health Impact

Depression, anxiety, and low self-esteem are more common in adolescents with obesity.

Metabolic Syndrome

A cluster of conditions including high blood pressure, blood sugar, and cholesterol levels.

How ObesiCare's AI System Works

Data-Driven Prediction

ObesiCare uses a Deep Learning Artificial Neural Network trained on real obesity data. It learns patterns from 16 health and lifestyle indicators to predict obesity risk across 7 classification levels.

Personalized Assessment

Our questionnaire covers gender, age, weight, height, dietary habits, physical activity, screen time, family history, and more. Every answer helps the AI model build an accurate risk profile.

Instant Risk Classification

After you submit, our AI instantly analyzes your data and classifies your obesity risk level with a confidence score, helping you understand your current health trajectory.

Actionable Recommendations

Based on your risk class, ObesiCare generates specific, practical recommendations for diet, exercise, and lifestyle changes you can start implementing immediately.
