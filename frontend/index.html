What is obesity?

More than body weight

Obesity is a health condition with excess body fat that may increase the risk of diabetes, blood pressure issues, heart disease, and reduced physical fitness.

What is adolescent obesity?

Growth stage with special risk

During teenage years, rapid physical and hormonal changes make nutrition, sleep, physical activity, and screen-time habits especially important for lifelong health.

Is ObesiCare a replacement for doctors?

No. ObesiCare is a screening and support tool, not a clinical diagnosis.

Can this help with prevention?

Yes. It highlights early risk signals and encourages healthy daily routines before severe issues develop.
